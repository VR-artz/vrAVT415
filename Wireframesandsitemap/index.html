  • Step 2-1: Go to Instagram Page

  • 2-1.1: On the "About & Contact" page locate the contact section.
  • 2-1.2: In the contact section locate the social media subcatagory.
  • 2-1.3: Click on the Instagram icon.
  • 2-1.4: Scroll threw the Instagram page.

  • Step 2-2: Go to Portfolio Page.

  • 2-2.1: From the "About & Contact" page return to the navigation panel and click the "portfolio" icon.
  • 2-2.2: On the "Portfolio" page click on the "Illustrations" section.
  • 2-2.3: Look through the Illustration gallery.
  • 2-2.4: Go back to the navigation panel and click on the "Portfolio" icon.
  • 2-2.5: On the "Portfolio" page click on the Design section.
  • 2-2.6: Look through the Design gallery.
